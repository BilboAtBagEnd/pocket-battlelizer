Class PocketBattles::Draft::StupidDraft
In: draft-1.0.rb
Parent: Object

Does a very stupid drafting:

  1. Shuffle all the units in the army
  2. Iterate through, picking and adding a unit to a formation until we‘ve either run out of formation points or army points.
  3. We‘ll stop forming a unit if we run into a unit that can‘t fit into the formation instead of, say, setting the formation to the side or something smrt like that. But it will tend to lead to more single units for soaking capabilities.
  4. At least we recycle units.
  5. On the other hand, we will always try to include aux units.
  6. At least we try our hardest not to create invalid draft armies.
